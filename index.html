FAQs

Got Questions?

We've answered the most common questions we get about our tree surgery and landscaping services. Can't find what you need? Just give Adam a call.

Ask Us Directly
Do I need permission to have a tree removed or reduced?
It depends on whether your tree is subject to a Tree Preservation Order (TPO) or is located within a Conservation Area. In those cases, you'll need to apply to your local council before any work can begin. Adam will advise you on this during the initial assessment, we're happy to guide you through the process.
What is Ash dieback and can you help?
Ash dieback (Hymenoscyphus fraxineus) is a serious fungal disease that has affected millions of ash trees across the UK. Infected trees can become structurally unstable and dangerous. Yes, we specialise in the safe management and removal of ash dieback-affected trees, from assessment and monitoring through to safe felling where necessary.
How much does tree surgery typically cost?
Costs vary depending on the size and species of the tree, the complexity of access, and the work required. Rather than give a price that might not apply to your situation, we always provide a free, no-obligation quote after assessing the job in person. Contact Adam today and we'll get a visit arranged promptly.
What areas do A.S Tree & Landscapes cover?
We are based in the UK and serve customers across a wide area. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, the quickest thing to do is give Adam a call on +44 7818 492860 and he'll be happy to confirm coverage for your area.
Will you clear up the waste after the job?
Absolutely. We take great pride in leaving every site as clean and tidy as we found it. All cuttings, brash, and debris are removed and disposed of responsibly as standard. You won't be left with a mess and that's a promise.
Can you help design and build a completely new garden?
Yes! Our landscaping services cover everything from ground clearance and preparation through to patio construction, fencing, turfing, and planting. Whether you want a simple lawn refresh or a full garden transformation, we can help. Get in touch to discuss your vision and we'll put together a tailored quote.
How long will my hedge trimming or patio project take?
Timescales vary by project size. A standard hedge trim can often be completed in a single day, while a full patio installation or garden landscaping project may take several days depending on the scope. Adam will give you a clear timeline as part of your free quote so you always know what to expect.
Are you insured to carry out tree surgery work?
Yes, A.S Tree & Landscapes is fully insured. We carry comprehensive public liability insurance to cover all aspects of our tree surgery and landscaping operations, giving you complete peace of mind when we work on your property. We're happy to provide proof of insurance on request.
